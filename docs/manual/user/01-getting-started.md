# Getting Started

This chapter covers the basics that apply to every user, regardless of role: signing in, what to do if you forget your password, and what to expect the first time you open the app.

## Signing in

Open your browser and go to **app.logisx.com**. You will see the login screen.

Enter your **username** and **password** — both are case-sensitive — and tap or click **Sign in**. After a successful sign-in, the app routes you to the home screen for your role:

- Drivers land on the driver app (`/driver`).
- Dispatchers and Super Admins land on the operations dashboard (`/dashboard`).
- Investors land on the investor dashboard (`/investor`).

If your credentials are wrong, you'll see a brief error message. After several failed attempts in a short window, the system temporarily blocks further attempts from your browser as a brute-force protection. Wait a few minutes and try again.

## Forgot your password

The app does not have a self-service password reset. Contact your administrator and ask them to reset it for you. They will set a temporary password and share it with you; sign in once and change it from your profile.

## Changing your password

From any logged-in screen, open your profile (usually a menu accessed from the sidebar or the upper-right of the screen) and use the **Change Password** option. You'll be asked for your current password and a new password. After changing, you remain signed in.

If you've forgotten your current password, you cannot change it yourself — contact your administrator for a reset.

## What browser to use

LogisX works best in current versions of:

- **Google Chrome** (recommended for desktop)
- **Microsoft Edge**
- **Safari** on Mac and iPhone
- **Firefox**

The driver app especially benefits from Chrome on Android or Safari on iPhone because GPS reporting is more reliable with these browsers.

**Internet Explorer is not supported.** If you only have IE, you'll need to install a modern browser.

## What happens when your session expires

If you don't use the app for an extended time, your session will quietly time out. The next action you take (clicking a link, submitting a form) will redirect you to the login screen. Sign back in and the app returns you to where you were trying to go.

This is intentional — it protects your account on shared devices.

## How the app stays current

LogisX uses real-time updates: when a load is assigned, a status changes, or a message arrives, the screens of every relevant user refresh automatically without anyone having to click reload. You won't see most refresh buttons on most screens, because the screens are always live.

The driver app's tabs (Loads, Status, Alerts, Kit, Messages) update the moment a dispatcher acts. The operations dashboard updates the moment a driver accepts a load or reports a new GPS position. The investor dashboard updates as new revenue arrives.

If you ever see information that looks stale, refresh the page — but this is rare in practice.
