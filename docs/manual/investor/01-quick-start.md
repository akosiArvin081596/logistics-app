# Quick Start

What you'll see when you sign in, the main navigation, and the typical monthly rhythm of being a LogisX investor.

## Signing in

Go to `app.logisx.com`. Enter your username and password. You land on the **Investor Dashboard** at `/investor`.

There's no sidebar; the dashboard is the home and the destination. You can navigate to **Trucks** to see your fleet detail.

## The hero header

The top of your dashboard:

- **Your profile photo** (you can update it).
- **Your name.**
- **"Performance overview"** as a subtitle.
- **Date range picker** — pick a month, a custom range, or all-time.
- **Download Report** button — generates a PDF for the selected range.
- **Refresh** button — force-reload the data (rarely needed since the dashboard updates in real time).

![Investor hero](screenshots/investor-hero.png)

## What's below the hero

A series of sections, top to bottom:

### Earnings section

The most-watched section. Shows:

- **Your Earnings for the selected month** — your 50% share of net profit.
- **Breakdown table** — Revenue minus Driver Pay minus Fixed Costs minus Trip Expenses = Net Profit, divided by 2.
- **Month selector** — jump between months.
- **All-time totals strip** — lifetime numbers.

![Investor earnings](screenshots/investor-earnings.png)

### Fleet Breakdown

A card per truck showing:
- Truck info.
- Current status (Active, Available, Maintenance).
- Currently-assigned driver.
- This month's revenue for that truck.

Click into a truck for the full record.

### Production section

Operational metrics:
- Miles driven.
- Loads completed.
- Utilization percentage.

### Cash Flow section

A line chart showing revenue over time.

### Asset section

Your trucks as assets with acquisition dates and book values.

### Document Portal

Every document you've signed during onboarding, plus any historical reports you've downloaded. You can view or re-download from here.

### Messages

Direct chat with LogisX operations.

## Your trucks view

Sidebar or top-nav → **Trucks**. You see only the trucks you own. Click into any truck for:

- VIN, plate, year, make, model.
- Mileage.
- Assigned driver (if any).
- Document list (registration, insurance, inspection).
- Owner ID (yours).
- Historical drivers (if applicable).

## The monthly rhythm

A typical month flow:

**Start of the month:**
- Sign in. Glance at the Earnings section to see the prior month's final numbers.
- Compare to your expectations.
- Download a PDF report for the prior month (for records).

**Mid-month:**
- Sign in. Check current-month accruing earnings.
- Look at the Production section — are loads being run?
- Watch for any maintenance status on your trucks.

**End of month:**
- Earnings for the month finalize as loads close out.
- Distribution typically processes in the first week of the following month.
- Your bank account should reflect your share.

## What auto-updates

The dashboard updates in real time:

- A load delivers → revenue updates.
- An expense gets approved → it shows in the breakdown.
- A driver is reassigned to one of your trucks → Fleet Breakdown updates.
- Status of one of your trucks changes → reflected immediately.

No need to refresh manually. The data is live.

## When to engage operations

You don't need to micromanage. LogisX handles operations. Reach out when:

- **A number on your dashboard doesn't make sense.** Ask for clarification.
- **You want a custom report** (date range, specific format).
- **You're considering adding to your fleet** or making a structural change.
- **You have a question about your documents** or contract.
- **You're confused about anything.** Easier to ask than to assume.

Don't message about routine operational stuff (which driver should run a load, what time a pickup should be). That's not your decision; let LogisX handle it.

## Profile and preferences

From the hero header, tap your profile photo to access:

- **Photo upload** — update your portrait.
- **Contact info** — update your email or phone.
- **Notification preferences** — adjust what you get emailed.
- **Change password** — set a new password.

Save changes to apply.

## A 5-minute monthly review

A minimal habit that catches issues:

1. **Sign in.**
2. **Look at the Earnings section** for the prior month. Is the number what you expected?
3. **Skim Fleet Breakdown** for each of your trucks. Anything in Maintenance?
4. **Check Production**. Miles and loads consistent with prior months?
5. **Download a PDF report** if you want it for your records.

Five minutes. Done. If you spot anything odd, message operations.

## What you don't have access to

- **Other investors' data.** You see only your fleet.
- **Driver personal info.** You see assigned driver names but not their full personal details.
- **Broker information.** Loads have broker references internally; you see revenue but not the broker name typically.
- **Company-wide financials.** Only your slice.
- **Operational controls.** No buttons to assign loads or move drivers.

This is intentional — investor read-only access lets you trust the numbers without being responsible for the operation.

## Common first-time questions

**"Why is my earnings $0 this month?"** Could mean no loads have been delivered yet on your trucks, or there's a data attribution issue. Open Fleet Breakdown — are your trucks active? If yes, message operations to investigate.

**"Why does the dashboard show a number different from what I expected?"** Look at the breakdown table. The formula is transparent. Compare each line to what you expected.

**"How do I see my agreement?"** Document Portal. The Master Participation Agreement and your Vehicle Lease should be there as signed PDFs.

**"When do I get paid?"** Distribution typically processes the first week of the following month. Direct deposit to the bank account you set up during onboarding.

**"What if I want to add a truck?"** Message operations. It involves a new Vehicle Lease and capital coordination — outside the app workflow.
