# Document Portal

Your Document Portal is where every signed agreement, every generated report, and every truck-related document you can access lives. This chapter is how to use it.

## Opening the portal

From your dashboard, scroll to the **Documents** section (or it may be in the sidebar / a separate page depending on your install).

You'll see categorized documents:

- **Onboarding documents** — signed during your initial onboarding.
- **Operating documents** — anything operations has shared with you.
- **Reports** — PDFs you've downloaded from the Download Report button.

## Your onboarding documents

After completing the investor onboarding flow, you have these signed documents on file:

### Master Participation & Management Agreement

The umbrella contract governing your relationship with LogisX. It defines:

- The profit-sharing structure (typically 50/50 of net profit).
- LogisX's management responsibilities.
- Your responsibilities as the truck owner.
- Dispute resolution.
- Termination clauses.

This is the single most important document — refer to it when contract questions arise. Open and re-read periodically.

### Vehicle Lease Agreement (one per truck)

For each truck you own, a separate Vehicle Lease documents:

- **Lessor** — you (the truck owner).
- **Lessee** — LogisX (operating the truck under contract).
- **Vehicle specifics** — VIN, year, make, model.
- **Lease terms** — duration, fees, conditions.
- **Inspection at handover.**

Each Vehicle Lease is signed separately. If you have multiple trucks, you have multiple Vehicle Leases.

### Exhibit A (one per truck)

The technical specification of each truck — VIN, equipment list, condition at handover, photos. Attached to the Master Agreement.

### W-9 Form

Your tax ID information. Used by LogisX for 1099 issuance at year-end.

### Other documents

Depending on your situation:

- **Buy-sell agreement** if you purchased a truck through LogisX.
- **Bank authorization** for direct deposit setup.
- **Personal guaranties** (if applicable).
- **Insurance certificates** specific to your trucks.

## Reading a document

Tap or click any document to open it inline in your browser. You can:

- **Zoom in** to read details.
- **Scroll** through multi-page documents.
- **Open in a new tab** for side-by-side comparison.

## Downloading a document

Each document has a download icon. Tap to save a copy to your device. Save to:

- **Local folder** for offline access.
- **Cloud backup** (Google Drive, iCloud, Dropbox).
- **Off-site copy** for redundancy.

You should have local copies of every document — don't rely solely on the platform.

## Why the Master Agreement matters

A few specific reasons to keep re-reading the Master Agreement:

1. **Profit split.** It defines exactly how your earnings are calculated. If you ever wonder why a number is what it is, the Agreement is the answer.

2. **Capital responsibilities.** It defines who pays for what — major repairs, replacement parts, etc. Important when a $5,000 transmission appears in your expenses.

3. **Termination terms.** If you ever want to exit, the Agreement defines the process. Months of notice, asset disposition, etc.

4. **Dispute resolution.** If something goes wrong and informal resolution doesn't work, the Agreement defines arbitration or other mechanisms.

5. **Amendments.** If you and LogisX have agreed to changes (different split, additional trucks, etc.), those should be documented as amendments.

## Where are the amendments?

If the Master Agreement has been amended, those amendments live in your Document Portal alongside the original. The original Agreement plus its amendments are the full operative document.

If you remember signing an amendment that isn't showing in your portal, message operations. It should be uploaded; if not, ask for a copy.

## Tax forms

At year-end:

**1099-NEC** — if you receive payments as an individual or single-member LLC, you'll get a 1099-NEC documenting the total payments for the year.

**K-1** (if applicable) — for some investor arrangements (partnership structure), you'll get a K-1 instead. Depends on your specific arrangement.

These tax forms appear in your Document Portal in January for the prior tax year. Use them for tax preparation.

## Historical reports

Every PDF you've downloaded via the Download Report button is preserved in your portal. You can re-download anytime.

If you generated a report for "March 2026" three months ago, you can re-download the same report. (Note: if data was retroactively changed after the original download, the regenerated report may differ slightly.)

## Sharing documents

Documents in the portal contain sensitive info. Share carefully:

- **Tax preparer:** yes, the 1099 / K-1 and any relevant reports.
- **Lawyer:** yes, the agreements when needed.
- **Bank:** yes, when applying for loans (proof of business income).
- **Other investors:** depends on your arrangement.
- **Strangers, online forums, social media:** never.

You decide who sees what. The portal doesn't track sharing.

## Document expiration and renewals

A few documents have expiration dates:

- **Truck registration** — annually, renewed by LogisX.
- **Truck insurance card** — renewed by LogisX.
- **Annual inspection** — annually.
- **Medical card** for drivers (not your responsibility).

You see them in the portal but you don't actively manage their renewal — LogisX does. If a document is past its expiration date by more than a few days, message operations.

## Adding documents

You don't directly upload documents to your portal. If you have something to add (e.g., proof of capital contribution, additional bank info), share it with operations and they'll add it on their end.

## When a document is missing

If you signed something during onboarding but it's not showing in your portal:

1. **Check categorization.** Some documents are in specific sections; scroll fully.
2. **Refresh the page.** Sometimes documents lag by a few seconds after upload.
3. **Message operations.** If it's truly missing, they can re-attach.

## Document accessibility

The portal works on mobile and desktop. PDFs render in your browser's native PDF viewer.

If a document doesn't render on mobile, download it and open in a PDF reader app. Adobe Acrobat Reader is free and works well.

## Privacy

Your portal is **your view only**. Other investors don't see your documents. Drivers don't see them. Only you and LogisX administrators have visibility.

The documents are stored:

- **On LogisX's server** (database + Google Drive backup).
- **In your portal** (downloadable copies).
- **In your local backups** (if you've downloaded).

The platform itself uses secure storage. Your local copies you manage as you would any sensitive file.

## Pro tips

- **Download everything in your first week.** Get local copies of every onboarding document.
- **Re-read the Master Agreement quarterly.** Not deeply — just a skim. Keeps the terms fresh.
- **Save tax forms immediately when they appear.** Don't risk a January server hiccup costing you tax-time access.
- **Organize your local copies** by year and type. Future-you will appreciate the structure.
