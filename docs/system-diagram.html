INVESTOR (users) │ │ owner_id │ ▼ TRUCKS (trucks) ────── assigned_driver ──────> DRIVER (users) │ │ │ unit_number │ driver_name │ │ ├──> legal_documents (truck_id) ├──> Job Tracking (Driver column) ├──> maintenance_fund (truck) ├──> expenses (driver) └──> compliance_fees (truck) ├──> messages (from/to) ├──> notifications (driver_name) ├──> driver_locations (driver) ├──> documents (driver) └──> load_responses (driver_name) Job Tracking (Google Sheets) │ │ Load ID │ ├──> Payments Table (matched by Load ID) ├──> Status Logs (appended on every status change) ├──> Job Summary (completed loads) ├──> documents (load_id) └──> messages (load_id) KEY LINK: Driver Name (text string) connects Google Sheets <-> SQLite │ ├── Google Sheets: Job Tracking "Driver" column (free text) ├── SQLite: users.driver_name (canonical) └── SQLite: trucks.assigned_driver (must match) All matched via LOWER() comparison — case-insensitive
Investor Dashboard Data Flow
Investor logs in │ ▼ SQLite: SELECT assigned_driver FROM trucks WHERE owner_id = {user.id} │ │ investorDriverSet = {"lesline johnson"} │ ├──> Google Sheets: Job Tracking filtered by investorDriverSet │ ├── Total Revenue (sum of rate column) │ ├── Completed Loads (status = delivered/completed) │ ├── Monthly Revenue (grouped by date) │ └── Avg Daily Revenue (last 30 days) │ ├──> Google Sheets: Payments Table filtered by matching Load IDs │ └── Paid Revenue, Payment Status │ ├──> SQLite: expenses WHERE driver IN investorDriverSet │ └── Fuel, Maintenance, Compliance, Other (itemized) │ ├──> SQLite: investor_config WHERE owner_id = {user.id} │ └── Purchase Price, Title Status │ └──> Calculated at request time: ├── Net Cash Flow = Revenue - Expenses ├── Owner Earnings = Net Cash Flow / 2 ├── Break-Even = (Purchase + $5K/truck) / Monthly Earnings ├── Business ROI = Net Revenue / Gross Revenue ├── Section 179 = Purchase Price (100%) ├── At-Risk Capital = (Purchase + Startup) - Net Revenue └── Per-Truck Est.
