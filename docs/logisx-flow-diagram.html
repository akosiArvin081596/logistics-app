Relationship Rules

RelationshipTypeRule
Investor → TrucksOne-to-ManyAn investor can own multiple trucks (via owner_id)
Truck → DriversMany-to-ManyA truck can have multiple drivers; a driver can be on multiple trucks. Guard: driver with active load cannot be reassigned.
Driver → LoadsOne-to-ManyA driver can have multiple loads, but only one active at a time (409 conflict if dispatched while busy)
Load → DocumentsOne-to-ManyA load can have multiple PODs/documents
Truck → Business ConfigOne-to-OneEach truck has its own purchase price, title status, maintenance fund, and fixed costs
