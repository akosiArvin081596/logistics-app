LogisX Investor Guide

Supplementary guide for new investors — not part of the legal agreement

Welcome to the LogisX Fleet!

We don't just move freight; we optimize assets. Here is what to expect as we launch your unit.

1. The Onboarding Phase (Days 1–7)

  • Tech Integration: We install our ELD and telemetry hardware.
  • Regulatory Suite: We file your Form 2290, secure your IRP Plates, and add the unit to our primary $1M Liability Policy.
  • Driver Matching: Our safety team vets and assigns a professional driver to your unit.

2. The "Net-60" Rhythm

To maintain a healthy cash flow and account for industry receivable aging:

  • Month 1: Your truck is out earning.
  • Month 2: We reconcile the loads, audits, and expenses.
  • Last Friday of Month 2: Your 50% share is deposited via ACH.

3. Maintenance: The Reserve Strategy

We deduct a $800 Maintenance Reserve and a $300 Contingency Buffer monthly.

  • Why? This ensures that when a tire blows or a sensor fails, the truck stays on the road. We handle the mechanics; you receive the reports.

4. Your Portal & Communication

You will receive a monthly "Birth-to-Death" Log. This is your itemized truth. It shows every dollar earned and every cent spent. For urgent asset inquiries, our management team is available via the owner-direct line.

Investor Onboarding Checklist

Ensure every asset unit is legally and operationally cleared before the first dispatch.

Phase 1: Compliance & Legal

  • Master Participation & Management Agreement: Signed by both parties.
  • Commercial Vehicle Lease Agreement: Signed by both parties.
  • W-9 Form: Completed and signed (required for 1099 issuance).
  • Investor Application: Completed and vetted.
  • Certificate of Insurance (COI): Proof of Physical Damage and Bobtail/Non-Trucking Liability naming LogisX Inc. as Additional Insured.

Phase 2: Asset Verification

  • Vehicle Title/Registration: Copy of front and back.
  • Form 2290 (HVUT): Proof of payment for current tax year.
  • Vehicle Delivery & Condition Receipt: Signed at handover.
  • Photos of Asset: 4 clear photos (Front, Back, Left, Right).
  • Maintenance Records: Last 90 days of service history.

Phase 3: Operational Integration

  • Schedule A (Banking/ACH): Verified routing and account numbers.
  • ELD Hardware Installation: Unit paired with fleet portal.
  • Decals/Placards: USDOT# and Company Branding applied.
  • Driver Assignment: Qualified driver assigned and briefed.
